- **Critical Metrics:** Security posture score >90/100, MTTR critical <7 days
- **Key Technology:** EPSS vulnerability scoring replaces CVSS-only approach

### Regulatory Compliance

- **Multi-Framework:** GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI-DSS, ISO 27001 tracking
- **Audit Trail:** >98% completeness target
- **Target:** >95% compliance across all frameworks

### Policy Enforcement

- **AWS Organizations:** SCP violation tracking, multi-account governance
- **Tagging:** >90% compliance target with mandatory tags
- **IaC Adoption:** >85% of resources managed via Terraform/CloudFormation
- **Shadow IT:** Automated detection of unapproved services

### Risk Management (EPSS Framework)

- **2025 Context:** >20,000 new CVEs in H1 2025, 35% with public exploits
- **EPSS Scoring:** Context-based risk (exploitability + business impact + exposure)
- **Attack Paths:** Visualization with blast radius analysis
- **Target:** MTTR critical <7 days, high <30 days

### AI/ML Governance

- **Market:** 78% of orgs use AI (up from 55% in 2023)
- **Challenges:** 80% cite explainability, ethics, bias as roadblocks
- **Requirements:** 100% bias checks, explainability (SHAP/LIME), EU AI Act compliance
- **Regulation:** EU AI Act high-risk system documentation mandatory

### Sustainability & Carbon

- **AWS Efficiency:** 4.1x more efficient than on-premises, up to 99% carbon reduction
- **AWS Target:** 100% renewable energy by 2025
- **CCFT:** Scope 1, 2, 3 emissions tracking with historical data to 2022
- **Target:** >15% YoY carbon footprint reduction

- **Market Growth:** USD 51.43B (2025) â†’ USD 84.67B (2030), 10.49% CAGR
- **AI Features:** GenAI for control mapping, agentic AI for compliance recommendations

**Tagging Strategy (Critical):**

**Governance Tags:**
- `Owner` - Resource owner
- `CostCenter` - Billing allocation
- `Environment` - dev/stg/prod
- `Project` - Project identifier
- `ManagedBy` - Terraform/CloudFormation/Manual

**Security Tags:**
- `Encrypted` - true/false
- `MFAEnabled` - true/false
- `PublicAccess` - true/false
- `ComplianceFramework` - GDPR/HIPAA/SOC2/PCI/ISO27001
- `FindingDate` - YYYY-MM-DD
- `EPSS` - 0.000-1.000 vulnerability score
- `AttackPath` - true/false

**AI/ML Tags:**
- `MLModel` - Registered/Unregistered
- `BiasCheck` - Passed/Failed
- `Explainability` - SHAP/LIME/None
- `Drift` - Normal/Warning/Critical
- `AIRisk` - Unacceptable/High/Limited/Minimal
- `ModelVersion` - X.Y.Z

**Sustainability Tags:**
- `CarbonMT` - Metric tons CO2e
- `StorageClass` - INTELLIGENT_TIERING/STANDARD/etc.
